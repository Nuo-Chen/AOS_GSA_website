Participate In The Community

❮ ❯

The Graduate Student Association is a student-led group that represents and acts on behalf of Graduate Students at in the Atmospheric and Oceanic Science department to facilitate dialogue between graduate students, faculty, and staff; to initiate and foster constructive development benefitting the graduate student body within the AOS Department and the University; to promote the voice and ideas of the graduate student body.

The GSA organize all kinds of events and meetings to promote all these ideas and reach out to the local community.

We are here to help if there is anything you need that you cannot find on our website.
